A Few Words About SYN

Born in Lagos, Nigeria, and now taking the world by storm, our Afrobeat sensation brings a fresh and vibrant energy to the global music scene. With influences ranging from Fela Kuti to modern hip-hop, the artist creates a unique sound that transcends borders.

The debut album "African Spirit" has been streamed over 100 million times worldwide and reached #1 on the World Music charts in 15 countries. The artist has been nominated for a Grammy and performed at major festivals across the globe.
